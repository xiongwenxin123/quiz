# Learning profile data attribution

The compressed files in this directory are generated by
`scripts/fetch_learning_profiles.py`. They are data assets, separate from the
application source code.

## English

- CEFR-J Vocabulary Profile 1.5 and CEFR-J Grammar Profile, Tono Laboratory,
  Tokyo University of Foreign Studies. Free research and commercial use with
  citation. Source: https://github.com/openlanguageprofiles/olp-en-cefrj
- Octanove Vocabulary Profile C1/C2, licensed CC BY-SA 4.0.

## Japanese

- Japanese Language Data project, licensed CC BY-SA 4.0.
  Source: https://github.com/jkindrix/japanese-language-data
- JLPT vocabulary classifications ultimately derive from Jonathan Waller's
  JLPT Resources under CC BY. They are community estimates, not official JLPT
  lists.
- The bundled grammar entries are marked draft by their source and must not be
  presented as native-speaker-reviewed.

## Spanish

- OpenSLR resource 21 Spanish Gigaword frequency list, licensed CC BY-SA 3.0
  US. Source: https://www.openslr.org/21/
- CEFR labels are deterministic frequency-band estimates created by this
  project. They are not official CEFR classifications.

Regenerate the assets to update sources or inspect the exact transformation.
